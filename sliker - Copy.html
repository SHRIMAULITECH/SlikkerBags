About Slikker Bags

Located in the heart of Navi Mumbai, Slikker Bags is your premier destination for high-quality American Tourister bags and accessories. We pride ourselves on offering an extensive collection of travel bags, backpacks, and luggage that combines style, durability, and functionality.

Our expert team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect bag for every journey, whether it's a business trip, family vacation, or daily commute. With years of experience in the industry, we understand what makes a great bag and are committed to providing exceptional customer service.
